# Class Description: QuizFragment

## 1. General Information
*   **Class Name:** `QuizFragment`
*   **Type:** `Fragment`
*   **Purpose:** Provides a 10-question test with a timer and score calculation.

## 3. Methods
**Method Name:** `startTimer`
*   **Function:** Starts a 30-second countdown for each question.

## 8. Simple Explanation
It's a **Digital Examiner**. It hands you a paper, starts a stopwatch, and grades you at the end.
